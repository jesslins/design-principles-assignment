About Treaty 1

At Lower Fort Garry, Treaty No. 1 was negotiated and signed in August 1871. The seven First Nations that signed the first of the numbered Treaties make up the Treaty One Nation. They are Anishinaabe (Ojibwe) and Nehiyaw (Cree).

Nations Involved

  • Peguis First Nation
  • Sagkeeng First Nation
  • Brokenhead Ojibway Nation
  • Roseau River Anishinaabe First Nation
  • Long Plain First Nation
  • Sandy Bay Ojibway First Nation
  • Swan Lake First Nation
